Cosmetic corner

Where beauty meets excellence!!

Cosmetic Corner is an e-commerce website that allows users to explore and purchase cosmetic products. The website will have the following features:

1. Login Page: A user-friendly login page where customers can log in to their accounts.
2. Home Page: A visually appealing home page that showcases products in a slider format, allowing customers to browse through the products.
3. Product Page: A dedicated page for each product, displaying details such as product description, price, and images.
4. Shopping Page: A page where customers can add products to their cart, view their wishlist, and proceed to checkout.
5. Cart and Wishlist: Customers can add/remove products from their cart and wishlist, and view the total cost.
6. Pricing: Display prices for each product, with options for discounts or promotions.

Technologies to be used: - Front-end: HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
